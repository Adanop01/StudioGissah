About

I'm Yahkoob P M, founder of Studio.Gissah. With extensive experience in architectural design, I specialize in creating spaces that blend functionality with aesthetic excellence.

My approach combines contemporary design principles with sustainable practices, ensuring each project not only meets client needs but also contributes positively to the environment and community. Based in Pathirippala, Palakkad, I serve clients across Kerala with innovative architectural solutions.
